Frequently Asked Questions

To reset your password, go to Settings > Change Password. Enter your current password and create a new one. You'll receive a confirmation email once the change is complete.

You can upgrade your subscription by going to Settings > Subscription Plans. Choose the plan that best fits your needs and follow the checkout process. Your new features will be activated immediately.

Data export is available in the Analytics section. Go to Analytics > Reports & Exports and select the data you want to export. You can choose from various formats including CSV, PDF, and Excel.

You can contact our support team by clicking the "Contact Support" button below or by emailing support@CoreAdmin.com. Our team typically responds within 24 hours on business days.
